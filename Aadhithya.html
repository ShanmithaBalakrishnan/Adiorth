Dr Aadhithya Priyadharshini Ashokkumar

ENGINEERING HEAD

Meet Priya, the innovative mind behind AdiOrth, alongside her partner Abishek. Priya, also known as Dr. Aadhithya Priyadharshini Ashokkumar, holds a PhD and is a graduate of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham in Coimbatore. She furthered her education with a doctoral degree from the University of Leeds, UK. Priya's expertise lies in strategic planning and problem-solving, which she applies diligently at AdiOrth, her brainchild that she's been nurturing since her graduation in December 2020.

During 2022-2023, Priya served as an Entrepreneur in Residence (EIR) at IIT-Madras. Currently, while managing her family's hospital, she's also exploring various opportunities within the healthcare sector. Despite hailing from a family of medical professionals and possessing an engineering background, Priya's passion lies in entrepreneurship. She recognized her calling and founded AdiOrth immediately after completing her studies.

Priya envisions herself as a serial entrepreneur with multiple thriving businesses. Beyond her professional pursuits, she indulges in hobbies like amateur motorcycling and gardening. Currently based in Coimbatore, Priya oversees the operations of AdiOrth, headquartered in the same city.
