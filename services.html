Additive Manufacturing:

At AdiOrth, we understand the benefits of Additive Manufacturing technologies and its application in building complex orthopedic devices. We are building AM orthopedic devices with markedly novel combination of biocompatible functionally graded materials. Currently available implants are susceptible to metal debris, impingement, fractures, squeaking, wear, free radical formation due to manufacturing process. Our solution is to leverage the benefits of AM and FGM, to solve these issues as well as produce AM orthopedic implants with enhanced designs, materials that have high availability in India.
